Golden Girls!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Golden Girls below. By clicking on a Golden Girl you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Golden Girl is hidden from you until you click on her.

Each time the game restarts, the value of the random number and the value of each Golden Girl resets.
